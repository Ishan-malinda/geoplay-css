Basic Shapes

Hover over the shapes to see them come to life!

Circle

A circle is a round shape with no corners. It's perfectly symmetrical!

Square

A square has four equal sides and four right angles. All sides are the same length!

Triangle

A triangle has three sides and three angles. There are different types like equilateral and right-angled!

Rectangle

A rectangle has four sides with opposite sides equal. It has four right angles!

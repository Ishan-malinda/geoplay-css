What are Coordinates?

Coordinates tell us exactly where a point is on the grid.

(x, y) = (3, 2)

The first number is left/right, the second is up/down!

Quadrants

The grid is divided into 4 sections called quadrants.

  • I: (+x, +y) - Top right
  • II: (-x, +y) - Top left
  • III: (-x, -y) - Bottom left
  • IV: (+x, -y) - Bottom right

Origin

The center point where x=0 and y=0 is called the origin.

All measurements start from here!

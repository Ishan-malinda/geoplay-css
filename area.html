Area & Perimeter

Discover how to measure the size and distance around shapes!

Rectangle Area

Area = Length × Width
Area = 15 × 10 = 150 square units

The space inside the rectangle.

Rectangle Perimeter

Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Width)
Perimeter = 2 × (15 + 10) = 50 units

The distance around the rectangle.

Circle Area

Area = π × radius²
Area ≈ 3.14 × 6² ≈ 113 square units

The space inside the circle.

Triangle Area

Area = (Base × Height) ÷ 2
Area = (15 × 13) ÷ 2 = 97.5 square units

The space inside the triangle.
